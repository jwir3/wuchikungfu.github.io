Wu Chi Kung Fu Association

Resolution

WCCBB-RES-2017-3

San Dan Rod Huus

Rules for the Ryan Carden Memorial Tournament
WHEREAS, We have recently had issues with miscommunication when scheduling the Ryan Carden Memorial Tournament; and
WHEREAS, The original purpose of the Ryan Carden Memorial Tournament was to honor Sifu Ryan Carden through hard work and fun; therefore, be it
RESOLVED, There will be a meeting of the College of Black Belts at every Ryan Carden Memorial Tournament to decide the location of the subsequent tournament.
RESOLVED, The head instructor of the school wishing to host the Ryan Carden Memorial Tournament must nominate his/her school at the aforementioned meeting, either in person or in writing. No proxy nominations will be allowed.
RESOLVED, The dates for the Ryan Carden Memorial Tournament for a given year be announced at least six (6) months in advance of the event.
RESOLVED, The location of the Ryan Carden Memorial Tournament will be decided by simple majority vote of those present at the aforementioned meeting.
RESOLVED, No changes to the location or dates of the Ryan Carden Memorial Tournament will be allowed after this meeting has concluded, except in the case of special request by the head instructor of the school scheduled to host the tournament.
RESOLVED, The content and events during the Ryan Carden Memorial Tournament are decided by the host school and the host instructor, except that at least one event or ceremony must specifically honor Sifu Ryan Carden.

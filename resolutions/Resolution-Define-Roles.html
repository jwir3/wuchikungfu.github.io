Wu Chi Kung Fu Association

Resolution

WCCBB-RES-2017-2

San Dan Scott Johnson

Amendment to More Clearly Define Roles of Executive Officers
WHEREAS, The Constitution of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association defines an Executive Council but does not specify individual responsibilities of members of this executive council; and
WHEREAS, The executive council has grown to a point where specific job requirements have become necessary; and
WHEREAS, The language in the Constitution of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association is confusing with regards to the difference between the 'Executive Council' and the 'College of Black Belts'; therefore, be it
RESOLVED, That the 'Executive Council' be formally renamed in the Constitution and By-laws of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association as 'College of Black Belts' and everywhere the term 'Executive Council' is currently used within the context of the Constitution and By-laws it is replaced with the term 'College of Black Belts'.
RESOLVED, That the term 'council member' be formally renamed in the Constitution and By-laws of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association as 'college member' and everywhere the term 'council member' is currently used within the context of the Constitution and By-laws it is replaced with the term 'college member'.
RESOLVED, That the Constitution of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association be amended to add a new Article, 'Article V: Officers', after current 'Article IV: Executive Council Members'. The content of this new article is attached as Exhibit A..
RESOLVED, That the Constitution of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association be amended to rename 'Article V: Meetings' be renamed to 'Article VI: Meetings'.
RESOLVED, That the Constitution of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association be amended to rename 'Article VI: Finance' be renamed to 'Article VII: Finance'.
RESOLVED, That the Constitution of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association be amended to rename 'Article VII: Ratification and Amendment' be renamed to 'Article VIII: Ratification and Amendment'.
RESOLVED, That the Constitution of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association be amended to rename 'Article VIII: By-laws' be renamed to 'Article IX: By-laws'.
RESOLVED, That the By-laws of the Wu Chi Kung Fu Association be amended to remove the phrase 'No voting by proxy will be allowed during this election' from 'Article II: Election of Officers, Section C'.
RESOLVED, That the current officers of the Association shall be assigned as follows: Chair: Rev. Gary D. Cecil, Vice-Chair: Loren Ness, Secretary: Scott Johnson, Treasurer: Justin Breitwieser, Keeper of the Scroll: Peter Ferguson, and Officer-At-Large: Paul Ough.
RESOLVED, That the remaining Officer-At-Large position be elected immediately following the approval of this resolution by a simple majority vote.
